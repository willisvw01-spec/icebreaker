HOW IT WORKS
  1. Get everyone in. One person taps Create a lobby and shares the 4-letter code. Friends enter a name + the code and tap Join. Everyone hits Ready up. Empty seats fill with AI drafters so it's always a 16-team bracket.
  2. Draft your team. Going in a snake order, each person picks 6 players — a left wing, center, right wing, two defensemen, and a goalie. Pick any player from any NHL team; once someone's taken, he's gone. 45 seconds a pick, or tap Auto-draft to let it pick for you.
  3. Watch it play out. All 16 rosters get seeded into a bracket and the games simulate based on each player's real-season stats. The bracket fills in round by round — tap any matchup to see the box score.
  4. Crown a champ. One roster survives to the top, and everyone gets a recap of how the league shook out — including who drafted a disaster.
